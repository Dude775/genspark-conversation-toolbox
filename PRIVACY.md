# Privacy Policy - Genspark Conversation Manager

**Last Updated: December 1, 2024**

## Data Collection
**We do NOT collect any user data.**

This extension:
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track user activity
- Does NOT use analytics
- Does NOT transmit data to external servers
- Does NOT share data with third parties

## Data Storage
All data stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local:
- **Language Preference**: Hebrew/English interface choice
- **Saved Conversations**: Conversations you save
- **No Cloud Storage**: Nothing uploaded to servers

## Permissions

**activeTab**: Access current Genspark.ai page when you click extension icon to extract conversation content for download and search.

**scripting**: Inject functionality into Genspark.ai pages to enable search, download, and save features. Runs only on genspark.ai domain.

**storage**: Save preferences and conversations locally on your device only.

**tabs**: Open saved conversations in new tabs and navigate between conversations.

**host_permissions (genspark.ai)**: Run extension only on Genspark.ai to extract and manage conversations. Does not access other websites.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Data Security
- All data remains on your device
- No transmission to external servers
- No cloud synchronization
- Full local control

## Your Rights
- Delete saved conversations anytime
- Change language preference anytime
- Uninstall extension to remove all local data

## Contact
GitHub: https://github.com/Dude775/genspark-conversation-toolbox/issues

## Compliance
Complies with Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, GDPR, and CCPA.

**Summary**: We don't collect, transmit, or share any user data. Everything stays on your device.
